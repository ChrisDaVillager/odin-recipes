Breakfast Burrito

Description

Have you ever been awaken from your slumber and felt like you just got hit by an 18 wheeler? Well fear not as this Chorizo Breakfast Burrito will help cure your mad hangover!

Ingredients

  • Cooking Spray
  • 3⁄4 pound chorizo sausage
  • 1⁄2 cup chopped red onion
  • 1 Green Chile Pepper (seeded and diced)
  • 4 Eggs
  • 4 Flour Tortillas
  • 1 Cup Shredded Cheese

Directions

  1. Spray frying pan with cooking spray
  2. Cook and stir chorizo over medium high heat until well browned
  3. Add onion and chile pepper
  4. Crack eggs and add chorizo to mixture [Reduce heat to medium-low]
  5. Warm flour tortillas for 30 seconds in microwave
  6. Add chorizo mixture to tortillas and roll up like a burrito
  7. ENJOY!
