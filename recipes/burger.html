Bronco Burger

Description

Are you heading out with your friends to watch the game but you don't want to pay those outrageous stadium prices? Here is the perfect tailgating burger!

Ingredients

  • 5 Jalapeno Peppers
  • 4 lbs Ground Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • 1⁄4 cup Steak Sauce
  • 1⁄4 cup White Onion (minced)
  • 1 tsp Tabasco
  • 1 pinch Dried Oregano
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 tsp Garlic Salt
  • 1⁄4 cup Fritos chips (crushed)
  • 8 Hamburger Buns
  • 8 slices Pepperjack Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat grill for high heat
  2. Roast Jalapeno Peppers until blackened on all sides
  3. In a large bowl, mix together jalapenos, ground beef, egg, steak sauce, onion, hot pepper sauce, oregano, Worcestershire sauce, garlic salt and chips
  4. Divide concoction into 8 balls and flatten into patties
  5. Grill patties for 10-15 minutes until desired results
  6. Place patties on bun
  7. Add accessories
  8. ENJOY!
